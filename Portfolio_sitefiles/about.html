My name is Mitchell Vlcek. I am working as a Power BI Analyst at Wilcox Industries in Newington, New Hampshire, and concurrently earning an associate's degree in IT through YCCC. I graduated from Marshwood High School in 2023, from which I ran cross country and track (indoor and outdoor) for all four years. Distance running has always been my specialty, and while I was never the fastest, I was the quiet, lead by example figure of the team, and was awarded a captaincy my senior year. Since graduating my physical activity has drifted more towards tennis, as I needed that competitive feeling again.


As for my more cerebral activities, I have passions for learning about history, geopolitics, economics, data science, marine biology, game design, creative writing, and music composition. I am currently learning to play the bass guitar and dabble with the piano on occasion. Luckily, one of those things actually has a high chance to yield a lucrative career, that being the data science, so that’s where I find myself today. At some point I hope to compose an album, write a book, and create a game with a few friends of mine, but those things will likely just be side projects while the data analytics take the front seat.


Some say life is food, I say life is music. If I’m not actively listening to music, something is playing in my brain in the background. My taste in genre is extremely wide, from thrash metal to R&B, orchestral soundtracks to Japanese instrumental jazz, just about anything with good composition or deep lyrics will get me excited.
