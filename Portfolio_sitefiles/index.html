Welcome to my review site!

I have decided to fill this site with reviews, be it music, games, movies, all things media. I figured it would be the best way to fill the site with content that I find actually valuable.


The end goal will be having this site be a library of reviews and have an ongoing tier-list of each of the previously mentioned categories. I will try and find the best way of making a tier-list structure within the site and build on it as I review more things.


I will aim to give actually in-depth analysis with my reviews, as opposed to just slapping a rating on and calling it a day.


I hope to make a shelf on the right side of the screen to list my most recent reviews in ascending order with links to their respective pages. I'll have to figure out a good navigation structure too when it comes to accessing the older stuff. I don't want to just keep pushing things further down the page infinitely.


With all that said, try the categories at the top and enjoy my ramblings!
